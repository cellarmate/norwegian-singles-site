Privacy Policy

Norwegian Singles

Effective date: January 1, 2025

Overview

Norwegian Singles ("the App") is developed by Sean Grapevine. This policy describes how the App handles your information. The short version: the App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required
  • No personal data is transmitted to any server
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks are included
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used

Data Storage

All data you enter into the App—including race results, VDOT values, saved workouts, and preferences—is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Apple HealthKit

The App may request access to Apple HealthKit to read workout data and write completed workouts. HealthKit data is handled exclusively by the iOS operating system and is never transmitted to external servers by the App. You can manage HealthKit permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

Apple Watch

The App can send structured workout plans to a paired Apple Watch using Apple's WorkoutKit framework. This communication occurs entirely on-device between your iPhone and Apple Watch. No workout data is transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App does not share data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Because the App collects no data, changes are expected to be infrequent.
