Privacy Policy

Norwegian Singles

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Overview

Norwegian Singles ("the App") is developed by Sean Grapevine / CellarMate LLC. This policy describes how the App handles your information. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the App's features and do not sell your data to anyone.

Data We Store on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Race results, VDOT history, and training paces
  • Weekly schedules, race plans, and ongoing training history
  • Saved workouts and preferences
  • Activity matching data and completion records

Third-Party Integrations

The App offers optional integrations with the following third-party services. These are entirely opt-in — you choose whether to connect them.

Strava

When you connect Strava, the App accesses your activity data (runs, distances, times) to match completed activities to your training plan. We store your Strava OAuth tokens on our server to maintain the connection. We do not access or store any data beyond what is needed for activity matching.

intervals.icu

When you connect intervals.icu, the App pushes structured workouts to your intervals.icu calendar and reads wellness data (training load metrics: CTL, ATL, TSB) to power training progression suggestions. We may also read and write sport settings (e.g. threshold pace) to enable pace targets on your watch. We store your intervals.icu OAuth tokens on our server to maintain the connection.

In-App Subscriptions

The App offers optional monthly subscriptions (the "Supporter" program). Subscriptions are entirely optional — all features of the App remain free whether you subscribe or not. Subscribing grants a cosmetic "thank you" state in the App; no features are unlocked by a subscription.

When you subscribe:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see your payment card, bank details, or any financial information.
  • Subscription status is managed on our behalf by RevenueCat, Inc. RevenueCat receives your anonymous app-generated user ID, device identifiers, and subscription events (product purchased, purchase date, expiration date, renewal status). This is used to validate your subscription and restore it across devices or reinstalls.
  • We do not send your name, email, or any personally identifying information to RevenueCat.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time: on iOS, Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. On Android, Play Store → Subscriptions. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Our Server

The App communicates with a secure server to facilitate third-party integrations. Our server stores:

  • OAuth tokens for Strava and intervals.icu (encrypted at rest)
  • Your app-generated user ID (not your name, email, or any personally identifying information)

Our server acts as a pass-through for API calls to Strava and intervals.icu. We do not store your workout data, activity data, or wellness data on our server — it is fetched on demand and delivered directly to your device.

Apple HealthKit

The App may request access to Apple HealthKit to read workout data and write completed workouts. HealthKit data is handled exclusively by the iOS operating system and is never transmitted to external servers by the App. You can manage HealthKit permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

Apple Watch

The App can send structured workout plans to a paired Apple Watch using Apple's WorkoutKit framework. This communication occurs entirely on-device between your iPhone and Apple Watch. No workout data is transmitted externally.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored on your device can be deleted at any time using the "Delete All Data" option in the App's Profile tab. This immediately removes all local data including training history, saved workouts, and preferences.

To delete server-side data (OAuth tokens and connection records), email sean@norwegiansingles.app with your request. Server-side data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

You can also disconnect Strava or intervals.icu at any time from the Profile tab, which revokes the App's access and removes the stored tokens from our server.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. We do not track your usage, location, or behavior within the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
