Norwegian Singles

Structured sub-threshold interval workouts based on the Norwegian Singles Method

About

Norwegian Singles generates interval workouts calibrated to your current fitness. Enter a recent race result, and the app calculates your VDOT using Jack Daniels' formula to determine precise sub-threshold training paces.

The Norwegian Singles Method adapts the elite Norwegian double-threshold model into three structured session types, each targeting the sub-threshold lactate zone (2.5–3.5 mmol/L) for maximum aerobic development with manageable recovery.

Session A, B, C

Short, medium, and long intervals—all at paces matched to the interval duration

Custom Workouts

Build your own intervals with specific distances or durations

Apple Watch

Send structured workouts to your watch with pace alerts for each interval

Free & Private

No ads, no account required, no data collection. Everything stays on your device.
