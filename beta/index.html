How it works
Tap the button above on your phone. If you have the Norwegian Singles app installed, it will open to a confirmation screen. Once enrolled, you'll see a new Heart Rate Training card on your Profile tab, plus an HR Zones panel on the Tools tab.

Don't have the app yet? Install it first, then come back to this page and tap Enroll.

Android not opening the app? Long-press the button, choose “Open link”, and Android's intent chooser will offer Norwegian Singles.
